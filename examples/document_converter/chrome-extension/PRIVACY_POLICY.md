# Privacy Policy for Document Converter

**Effective Date**: December 1, 2025

## Overview
Document Converter is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension processes all documents and images entirely within your browser, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

## Data Collection and Usage

### What We DO NOT Collect
- We do **NOT** collect any personal information
- We do **NOT** upload your files to any server
- We do **NOT** track your browsing activity
- We do **NOT** share any data with third parties
- We do **NOT** use cookies or analytics

### Local Processing Only
All document and image processing happens entirely in your browser:
- Files you open remain on your device
- All conversions (PDF, Word) are performed locally
- Image editing operations are processed client-side
- No data is transmitted over the network

## Permissions Explanation

The extension requests the following permissions:

### 1. **storage**
- **Purpose**: To save your preferences and settings (such as last used zoom level)
- **Data Stored**: Only user preferences and application settings
- **Location**: Chrome's local storage on your device only

### 2. **unlimitedStorage**
- **Purpose**: To handle large documents and images without storage limitations
- **Usage**: Allows processing of large PDF files and high-resolution images
- **Location**: All data remains in your browser's temporary memory

## Camera Access
If you use the camera capture feature:
- Camera access is requested only when you click the camera button
- Images captured are processed locally in your browser
- No camera data is stored or transmitted

## Data Retention
- Files are only kept in memory while the extension is open
- All data is cleared when you close the extension
- No persistent file storage is maintained

## Third-Party Services
This extension does **NOT** use any third-party services, APIs, or analytics tools.

## Children's Privacy
This extension does not knowingly collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's listing and within the extension itself.
